# Glossary — ADM-11013 International Business

Acronyms and specialised terms used across the course, grouped by theme for easier study. A term appears in multiple groups only when its meaning varies by context.

For the full explanation, follow the link to the relevant topic file. This glossary gives one-line definitions only.

---

## Trade agreements and integration


| Term                           | Definition                                                                                              | Topic                                                    |
| ------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- |
| **FTA**                        | Free Trade Area — member countries remove internal trade barriers but keep independent external tariffs | [T01](topics/T01%20-%20Economic%20Integration.md)        |
| **Customs Union**              | FTA + common external tariff against third countries; no internal rules of origin needed                | [T01](topics/T01%20-%20Economic%20Integration.md)        |
| **Common Market**              | Customs Union + free movement of factors of production (labour, capital)                                | [T01](topics/T01%20-%20Economic%20Integration.md)        |
| **Economic Union**             | Common Market + coordination of economic policies (exchange rate, inflation, fiscal deficit)            | [T01](topics/T01%20-%20Economic%20Integration.md)        |
| **Monetary Union**             | Economic Union + common currency circulating among members                                              | [T01](topics/T01%20-%20Economic%20Integration.md)        |
| **Political / Complete Union** | Union functions as a single country with central government (USA, Mexico)                               | [T01](topics/T01%20-%20Economic%20Integration.md)        |
| **Factors of production**      | Labour, capital, (sometimes) land and entrepreneurship — move freely in a Common Market                 | [T01](topics/T01%20-%20Economic%20Integration.md)        |
| **Fiscal Union**               | Full harmonisation of taxation across members (EU is not one)                                           | [T01](topics/T01%20-%20Economic%20Integration.md)        |
| **NAFTA**                      | North American Free Trade Agreement (1994–2020), 22 chapters                                            | [T02](topics/T02%20-%20NAFTA%20and%20TMEC.md)            |
| **TMEC / T-MEC / USMCA**       | Tratado México-EEUU-Canadá — the 2020 successor to NAFTA                                                | [T02](topics/T02%20-%20NAFTA%20and%20TMEC.md)            |
| **Mercosur**                   | Southern Common Market (Argentina, Brazil, Paraguay, Uruguay + others)                                  | [T01](topics/T01%20-%20Economic%20Integration.md)        |
| **Eurozone**                   | The subset of EU members using the euro — a Monetary Union                                              | [T01](topics/T01%20-%20Economic%20Integration.md)        |
| **WTO**                        | World Trade Organization — multilateral trade rules, dispute resolution                                 | [T03](topics/T03%20-%20US%20Agricultural%20Subsidies.md) |


## Tariffs, rules of origin, and customs codes


| Term                           | Definition                                                                                                        | Topic                                                                                                      |
| ------------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Tariff**                     | Tax on imports (or less commonly, exports)                                                                        | [T04](topics/T04%20-%20Tariffs%20Policy.md)                                                                |
| **Ad valorem**                 | Tariff as a percentage of value                                                                                   | [T04](topics/T04%20-%20Tariffs%20Policy.md)                                                                |
| **Specific tariff**            | Fixed amount per unit                                                                                             | [T04](topics/T04%20-%20Tariffs%20Policy.md)                                                                |
| **Variable tariff**            | Adjusts with reference prices                                                                                     | [T04](topics/T04%20-%20Tariffs%20Policy.md)                                                                |
| **Official price list**        | Government-set reference prices for tariff calculation                                                            | [T04](topics/T04%20-%20Tariffs%20Policy.md)                                                                |
| **Compound / combined tariff** | Mix of ad valorem and specific components                                                                         | [T04](topics/T04%20-%20Tariffs%20Policy.md)                                                                |
| **Non-tariff barrier (NTB)**   | Regulation, quota, license, subsidy, or standard that restricts trade without being a tariff                      | [T04](topics/T04%20-%20Tariffs%20Policy.md)                                                                |
| **Import quota**               | Quantitative limit on imports of a good                                                                           | [T04](topics/T04%20-%20Tariffs%20Policy.md)                                                                |
| **VER**                        | Voluntary Export Restraint — exporting country limits its own exports                                             | [T04](topics/T04%20-%20Tariffs%20Policy.md)                                                                |
| **Import license**             | Government permission required to import                                                                          | [T04](topics/T04%20-%20Tariffs%20Policy.md)                                                                |
| **Subsidy**                    | Government transfer lowering producer costs (can act as NTB)                                                      | [T03](topics/T03%20-%20US%20Agricultural%20Subsidies.md)                                                   |
| **Infant-industry argument**   | Justification for protecting nascent industries until they mature                                                 | [T04](topics/T04%20-%20Tariffs%20Policy.md)                                                                |
| **Retaliation / trade war**    | Countries imposing reciprocal trade barriers                                                                      | [T04](topics/T04%20-%20Tariffs%20Policy.md)                                                                |
| **Price floor**                | US ag subsidy #1 — guaranteed minimum price for farmers                                                           | [T03](topics/T03%20-%20US%20Agricultural%20Subsidies.md)                                                   |
| **Set-aside / Fallow**         | US ag subsidy #2 — payment for not producing (every 7–9 years)                                                    | [T03](topics/T03%20-%20US%20Agricultural%20Subsidies.md)                                                   |
| **Technical assistance**       | US ag subsidy #3 — agricultural research and extension services funded for farmers                                | [T03](topics/T03%20-%20US%20Agricultural%20Subsidies.md)                                                   |
| **HCC / HS**                   | 8-digit Harmonized Customs Code (first 6 digits are the global Harmonized System)                                 | [T02](topics/T02%20-%20NAFTA%20and%20TMEC.md) / [T07](topics/T07%20-%20Export%20Documents%20and%20Flow.md) |
| **Fracción arancelaria**       | Spanish for tariff classification / HCC entry                                                                     | [T02](topics/T02%20-%20NAFTA%20and%20TMEC.md)                                                              |
| **Category A / B / C / C+**    | NAFTA tariff-removal categories: A=immediate, B=5-year, C=10-year, C+=15-year (sensitive goods)                   | [T02](topics/T02%20-%20NAFTA%20and%20TMEC.md)                                                              |
| **Rules of origin**            | Criteria determining whether a good qualifies as regional under an FTA                                            | [T02](topics/T02%20-%20NAFTA%20and%20TMEC.md)                                                              |
| **RVC**                        | Regional Value Content — percentage of value added regionally                                                     | [T02](topics/T02%20-%20NAFTA%20and%20TMEC.md)                                                              |
| **TV**                         | Transaction Value (FOB basis) used in Method 1 of RVC                                                             | [T02](topics/T02%20-%20NAFTA%20and%20TMEC.md)                                                              |
| **NC**                         | Net Cost — used in Method 2 of RVC                                                                                | [T02](topics/T02%20-%20NAFTA%20and%20TMEC.md)                                                              |
| **VNM**                        | Value of Non-originating Materials (from outside the FTA)                                                         | [T02](topics/T02%20-%20NAFTA%20and%20TMEC.md)                                                              |
| **FOB**                        | Free On Board — Incoterm; also the valuation basis for TV method                                                  | [T02](topics/T02%20-%20NAFTA%20and%20TMEC.md) / [T08](topics/T08%20-%20Incoterms.md)                       |
| **Maquiladora**                | Mexican border-assembly program (est. 1965) allowing duty-free foreign inputs; tariff only on Mexican value added | [T02](topics/T02%20-%20NAFTA%20and%20TMEC.md) / [T10](topics/T10%20-%20Modes%20of%20Entry.md)              |
| **IGI**                        | Impuesto General de Importación — Mexico's general import duty (0 under TMEC)                                     | [T07](topics/T07%20-%20Export%20Documents%20and%20Flow.md)                                                 |
| **IVA**                        | Impuesto al Valor Agregado — Mexico's VAT (16%), still applies on imports                                         | [T07](topics/T07%20-%20Export%20Documents%20and%20Flow.md)                                                 |
| **DTA**                        | Derecho de Trámite Aduanero — customs processing fee in Mexico                                                    | [T07](topics/T07%20-%20Export%20Documents%20and%20Flow.md)                                                 |
| **PRV / Prevalidación**        | Pre-validation of customs documents                                                                               | [T07](topics/T07%20-%20Export%20Documents%20and%20Flow.md)                                                 |
| **Régimen A1**                 | Mexican customs regime for definitive import                                                                      | [T07](topics/T07%20-%20Export%20Documents%20and%20Flow.md)                                                 |

| **Joint Venture (JV)**                 | Shared-ownership cross-firm collaboration                                                    | [T10](topics/T10%20-%20Modes%20of%20Entry.md) / [T14](topics/T14%20-%20Joint%20Ventures.md)     |

| **Strategic behavior (Knickerbocker)** | FDI as imitative oligopolistic response                                                      | [T12](topics/T12%20-%20FDI%20Theories.md)                                                       |

| **Tacit / firm-specific capabilities** | Capabilities embedded in organisation, not transferable via contract                         | [T11](topics/T11%20-%20FDI%20vs%20Licensing.md)                                                 |
